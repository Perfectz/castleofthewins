# Screenshot Manifest

## Staging Notes

- Spell Study is staged on a level-4 rogue so the draft includes both newly unlocked and older skipped spells.
- Magic Book and Field Tray are staged around `Light` because it is one of the clearest examples of a useful spell that previously needed better explanation and stronger differentiation.
- Each state was captured in both mobile and desktop viewports before and after the spell changes.
