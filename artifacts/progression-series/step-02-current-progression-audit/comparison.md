# Comparison

## Before

- Floor reward previews were too generic to support planning. The journal reduced meaningful differences to labels like `Relic reward`, `Rumor reward`, and `Boon choice`.
- `Quick Hands` appeared in the rogue perk draft as a promise rather than a real system hook.
- `Greedy Purse` appeared in relic drafts as a concept rather than a live tradeoff.

## After

- Floor reward previews now tell the player what kind of payout shape to expect, including whether the objective leads to relic drafting, boon drafting, or rumor intel.
- `Quick Hands` now carries a real rogue-facing payoff: light utility loot becomes frictionless and safer to grab after the objective is already clear.
- `Greedy Purse` now has a true greed identity: more post-objective gold at the cost of faster pressure escalation.

## Net Result

This step did not redesign progression. It repaired two trust-breaking dead choices and made one of the weakest information layers actually useful, which is the correct scale for a current-state audit pass.
