# Linked Post

I completed a deep current-state audit of `castleofthewinds` progression and shipped three narrow fixes instead of jumping straight to redesign.

What already works:
- Build assembly is genuinely deep.
- Class identity is clear.
- The extract-or-greed reward loop is one of the game's best progression beats.

What was weak:
- Reward forecasting was too generic.
- Some choice descriptions were carrying too little mechanical meaning.
- Two visible progression entries were underwired enough to hurt trust in the pool.

What changed:
- `Quick Hands` now makes light utility looting safer and lower-pressure after the objective is clear.
- `Greedy Purse` now gives 25% more greed gold and adds extra pressure in return.
- Objective reward previews now tell players what kind of payout shape they are actually chasing.
