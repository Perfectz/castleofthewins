# Linked Post

Step 00 of the progression series is complete: a baseline atlas of the current game's ability, spell, perk, relic, boon, unlock, and build-progression systems.

This was not a redesign pass. It was a code-backed evidence pass with Playwright captures in mobile and desktop viewports, plus after-side atlas boards that turn the raw screenshots into a readable progression map.

Main takeaway: the current game already has a multi-layer progression stack. Growth is coming from creation, level-up, spellbooks, objective rewards, town services, mastery, contracts, commendations, and funded town unlocks, not from XP alone. That is a strong foundation, but it is also spread across many surfaces, which means the next step should focus on clarity and system emphasis rather than adding new layers immediately.

- `screenshots/after/` for the annotated atlas boards
