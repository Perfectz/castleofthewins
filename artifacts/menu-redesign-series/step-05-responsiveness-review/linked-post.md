# Teaser

Step 05 of the menu redesign series focused on responsiveness, but not in the usual frame. Instead of chasing abstract speed, the review tightened focus visibility, selection contrast, blocked-state clarity, and spell handoff timing so the menu system feels more confident in real play.

## Follow-Up Blurb

The most useful responsiveness fixes were not flashy. They were small hierarchy moves that made the game answer input more clearly: stronger focus, stronger active states, and one tiny timeout removed from the spell-cast path.

## Headline Ideas

- Responsiveness Is Confidence, Not Just Speed
- Tightening a Tactical UI Without Adding Noise
