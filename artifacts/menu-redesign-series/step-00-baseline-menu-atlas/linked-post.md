# Teaser post

Step 00 in the menu redesign series is live: a baseline atlas of the current UI, captured from the running game in mobile and desktop viewports. Before any redesign comps, the team now has a publication-ready record of title flow, town services, dungeon HUD, utility surfaces, progression overlays, and fail states.

# Follow-up blurb

The most useful part of this step is not just the raw screenshots. It is the annotated study boards and written report that show how information is currently split across board, feed, map, stats, journal, briefing, and menus, which gives the next redesign step a much stronger starting point.
