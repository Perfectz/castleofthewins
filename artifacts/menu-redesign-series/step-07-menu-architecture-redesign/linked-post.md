# Teaser

Step 07 moves from local menu polish to system shape. I replaced three overlapping support destinations with one shared `Field Guide` prototype and captured the before/after flows in mobile and desktop runtime.

## Follow-Up Blurb

The interesting part of this step is not the label change. It is the architecture shift: `Mission`, rules/help, and the old journal now behave like sections of one support surface instead of unrelated modals. That makes the menu system easier to parse without pushing more text into the HUD.
