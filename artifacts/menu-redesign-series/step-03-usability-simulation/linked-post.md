# Teaser

Step 03 in the menu redesign series focused on likely confusion, but refused the usual fix of adding more UI text. The live patch instead made the first town action more literal and the system menu easier to scan through grouping alone.

# Follow-up blurb

Using Playwright evidence from mobile and desktop captures, this step simulated five player lenses and shipped two small improvements: `Services` and `North Road` for first-town clarity, plus a grouped `Adventure Menu` that separates run, saves, and system controls. It is a case study in reducing confusion with less interface, not more.
