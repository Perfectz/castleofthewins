# Step 08: Executive Roadmap and Final Pass

This capstone step reviews the whole menu redesign series as one body of evidence instead of one more isolated prompt. It decides what should survive into a practical Phase 1 roadmap, what should be deferred until more validation exists, and what should be rejected for now.

It also ships one final justified polish bundle: the support language around `Field Guide` and the old `Guide` entry was still too overlapping after step 07, so this step renames the rules/help entry and section to `Rules`. That keeps the architecture shift intact while removing the cleanest remaining ambiguity.

Why this matters:

- it turns a run of exploratory UI prompts into a real decision document
- it captures which changes are high-confidence enough to keep
- it leaves behind a clean teaching and blog-ready artifact trail rather than a pile of disconnected experiments
