# Teaser

The capstone step for the menu redesign series is live. It turns eight rounds of screenshots, UX critique, engineering review, and shipped deltas into one decisive roadmap and one last tiny polish.

## Follow-Up Blurb

The final lesson was simple: not every good idea should survive. The strongest wins were clearer labels, better hierarchy, stronger focus states, safer modal behavior, and a unified `Field Guide`, while larger town and reward changes remain deferred until they earn stronger evidence.
